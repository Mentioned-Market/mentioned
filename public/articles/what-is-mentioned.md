## What Are Mention Markets?

**Mention markets**, pioneered by Mentioned, are a specialized form of prediction markets that focus exclusively on language and mentions. Unlike traditional prediction markets that focus on yes/no outcomes, **Mentioned markets** allow traders to speculate on the specific vocabulary used in:

- Political speeches and debates
- Tech company keynotes (Apple, Google, Tesla)
- Podcast episodes (Joe Rogan, Lex Fridman)
- Corporate earnings calls
- Sports broadcasts and commentary
- YouTube videos and content
- Gaming patch notes
- TV shows and streaming content
